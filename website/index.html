Common Questions

Is a computer required?

No. SwiftSigner Pro runs entirely on your iOS device. You can import, sign, and install apps without ever touching a PC or Mac.

Do I need my own certificate?

Yes, for long-term use perfectly, you should have your own p12 certificate and provisioning profile. However, we support partial signing with public certificates too.

Is it safe?

Absolutely. All signing operations happen locally on your device. Your certificates never leave your secure storage unless you enable cloud sync explicitly.
